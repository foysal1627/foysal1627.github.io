FAQ

What services do you offer?
I offer ethical hacking, web application penetration testing, network security assessments, OSINT investigations, vulnerability scanning, and ongoing cybersecurity consulting. Whether you need a one-time audit or a long-term security partner, I can help.
How do I get started?
Simply reach out via WhatsApp, Facebook, or other contact method below. I'll respond within 24 hours to discuss your requirements, define scope, and provide a quote. Everything starts with a free initial consultation.
Is ethical hacking legal?
Yes — 100% legal when performed with explicit written authorization from the system owner. Before any engagement, I require a signed scope-of-work agreement. I never test systems without proper permission. All my work is fully compliant with applicable laws.
How long does a pentest take?
Timeline depends on scope. A basic web app audit takes 3–5 days. A full network penetration test for a small business takes 1–2 weeks. OSINT investigations typically 2–4 days. I'll give you a precise timeline before we begin — no surprises.
Do you provide a detailed report?
Absolutely. Every engagement ends with a professional PDF report. It covers all discovered vulnerabilities, their risk rating (Critical / High / Medium / Low / Informational), proof-of-concept evidence, business impact analysis, and step-by-step remediation guidance. A follow-up verification scan is also available.
What is your pricing?
Pricing varies based on scope, complexity, and duration. I offer competitive rates tailored to individuals, startups, and enterprises. Contact me for a free quote — I'll assess your needs and provide a fair, transparent price with no hidden charges.
