07 — FAQ
COMMON QUESTIONS
What services do you offer?
I offer ethical hacking, web application penetration testing, network security assessments, OSINT investigations, vulnerability scanning, and ongoing cybersecurity consulting. One-time audit or long-term partner — I can help.
How do I get started?
Reach out via WhatsApp, Facebook, or email. I'll respond within 24 hours to discuss requirements, define scope, and provide a quote. Everything starts with a free initial consultation.
Is ethical hacking legal?
100% legal when performed with explicit written authorization. I require a signed scope-of-work agreement before any engagement. All my work is fully compliant with applicable laws.
How long does a pentest take?
A basic web app audit takes 3–5 days. A full network pentest takes 1–2 weeks. OSINT investigations: 2–4 days. I give a precise timeline before we begin — no surprises.
Do you provide a detailed report?
Every engagement ends with a professional PDF report: all vulnerabilities, risk rating (Critical / High / Medium / Low), PoC evidence, business impact analysis, and step-by-step remediation guidance.
What is your pricing?
Pricing varies by scope and complexity. Competitive rates for individuals, startups, and enterprises. Contact me for a free quote — transparent pricing, no hidden charges.
